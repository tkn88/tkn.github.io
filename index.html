Our Services

Why we are so different

Transcription

The lingual messages in the audio or video file are transcribed to written words. All the transcription work is completed by local experienced linguists.

Desktop Publishing(DTP)

Transokin provides a wide range of multi-lingual desktop publishing (DTP) services, including the format adjustment and drawing design for journal, user’s manual, technical document, promotion material, online document and training material.

Copywriting

Mainly used by advertising and marketing professionals, it refers to the perfect transfer of the messages from one language to another language, and at the same time the intention, style and tone are kept, and such process is far beyond the scope of simple marketing translation.

Multimedia

Transokin provides high-quality voice dubbing and voiceover localization services, including: Multi-lingual professional audio dubbing; Multi-lingual voice over; Multi-lingual subtitle; Voice script translation.

Machine Translation

With the development of statistical technology and advanced algorithm, the use of machine translation is wider and wider, especially for some large projects in which price comes first, machine translation can be used, and then manual editing is performed.

Professional Translation
